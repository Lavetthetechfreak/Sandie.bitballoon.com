Who Am I

Hello there,my name is Sandra Omondi,Who am I?well I never seem to have a solid
answer to that but in a nutshell,I love to think of myself as a fashion police.
Fashion is my voice in this confusing world.

Fashion has always been my thing since childhood,well I believe that people express themselves
through what they wear.I am a writter,blogger and a stylist.I started blogging just recently
when I discovered that most ladies find it hard to put together what they have in there
wardrobe and look sexy.

I always try out new things and try to stay away from my comfort zone.Its been a walk
across a lego path but an interesting one.My work focuses on guiding people on what to wear
which cuts across all shapes and sizes.I would say that Blogging has been a blessing to me.
